Requirement Assessments

Please be advised of the following procedures for the submission of these requirements.

1. Monthly Performance Assessment

  • For all Field Team members (Managers down to Merchandisers/PMS).
  • Effective June 15, 2026: Must be attached to DTRs.
  • Basis: Previous month (e.g., May 2026 for the June 15 cut-off).
  • Must be signed by the person evaluated and submitted to Head Office.

2. Exit Clearance

  • Required for all resigned Disers and PMS.
  • Must attach the last inventory report (written or printed via APP/Google report).
  • Effective Date: June 5, 2026.
  • Managers/Supervisors are responsible for ensuring compliance.
