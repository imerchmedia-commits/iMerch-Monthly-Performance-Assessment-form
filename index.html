Assessments Summary per Supervisor

1. Monthly Performance Assessment

  • 1.a Submit Monthly Performance Assessments for all team members.
  • 1.a All Field Team members need to submit the Monthly Performance Assessment of their respective team members from Managers to Merchandising Operations Supervisors / Trade Supervisors / Field Coordinators/ Team Leaders and to Merchandisers and PMS.
  • 1.b These performance evaluation MUST be attached to your DTRs effective June 15, 2026. These should be based on your Monthly coverage plan. For this June 15 cut-off the basis of the evaluation is the previous month which is May 2026.
  • 1.c Monthly performance assessment will be based on the previous month and your coverage plan. The assessment sheets must be signed by the person evaluated and submitted to the Head Office for the checking of the SBG1 Managers/ Head. All copies will be transmitted to HR for 201 filing.
